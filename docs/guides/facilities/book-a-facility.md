# {{ page.title }}

*Browse your community's facilities, pick an open time slot, and confirm a booking in a few taps.*

## Who this is for

Residents with an active account in a community that has at least one bookable facility.

## Before you start

- You must be logged in as a Resident with an active membership in your community.
- Your community must have at least one facility configured for resident booking. If you see no facilities, contact your Property Manager.
- Some facilities require a signed contract before a booking can be confirmed (for example, a banquet hall). You will be informed at the time of booking.

## Steps

### 1. Open Facilities

Tap **Facilities** in the bottom navigation. The page loads a card grid showing every facility your community has made available for booking. Each card shows:

- The facility name and type
- Pricing (**Free**, **SAR [n] / session**, or **SAR [n] / hour**)
- Capacity (maximum number of residents per slot)
- A **Contract required** badge if a signed contract is needed before your booking is confirmed

### 2. Pick a facility

Tap **Book** on the facility card you want to reserve.

### 3. Select a date

A date strip appears showing the next 7–14 days. Tap the day you want to visit. Days on which the facility is closed are not selectable.

### 4. Choose a time slot

The slot grid opens below the date strip, grouped by time of day:

| Group | Label |
|-------|-------|
| Early morning and morning hours | **Morning** |
| Midday hours | **Afternoon** |
| Late hours | **Evening** |

Each slot shows one of three states:

| State | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Available** | The slot is open — tap to select it |
| **Booked** | The slot is fully reserved — you cannot tap it |
| **Closed** | The facility does not operate at this time — you cannot tap it |

If every slot for the selected date is booked, the page shows "All slots are booked for this date." Join the waitlist if that option appears, or select a different date.

### 5. Confirm the booking

Tapping an available slot opens the **Confirm Booking** sheet at the bottom of the screen. Review the details:

- **Date** — the day you selected
- **Time** — the start and end time of the slot
- **Duration** — the length of the session
- **Price** — the per-slot charge (or Free)
- **Total** — the amount that will be recorded

A note reminds you that confirmation will be sent to your registered mobile number and email. Tap **Confirm** to complete the booking, or tap **Cancel** to go back and choose a different slot.

### 6. Booking confirmed

After you tap **Confirm**, a success notification appears: "Booking confirmed! You will receive a confirmation shortly." The slot you booked is immediately removed from the available grid so no other resident can select it.

**If the facility requires a contract:** your booking is created with a pending status. You will receive instructions on how to sign the required contract to activate the booking.

## What you'll see

After a successful booking:

- The slot you reserved shows as **Booked** in the grid if you return to the same date.
- A confirmation notification is sent to your registered mobile number and email.
- The booking appears in your bookings history (coming in a future release).

## Common issues

- **"This slot is no longer available — please choose a different time."** Two residents tried to book the same last slot at the same moment; the other resident's booking was confirmed first. Tap **Pick Another Slot** to return to the grid and choose a different time.
- **No facilities appear on the page.** Your community has not yet enabled any bookable facilities. Contact your Property Manager.
- **A slot shows as Closed on a day you expected it to be open.** The facility's operating hours do not include that slot. Check with your Property Manager if you think this is incorrect.
- **"This facility requires a signed contract."** Your booking is saved as pending. Follow the contract-signing steps sent to you to confirm the reservation.
- **"Something went wrong. Please try again."** A temporary error occurred. Refresh the page and repeat the steps. If the problem persists, contact support.
