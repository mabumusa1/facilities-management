# Sign in to your account

*Sign in with your email and password. The platform now takes you to the right landing page for your role automatically.*

## Who this is for

Anyone with an account — admins, managers, owners, residents, service technicians, and gate officers.

## Before you start

- You need your **email address** and **password**.
- Your account must be active. Locked or deactivated accounts cannot sign in.

## Steps

1. Open the platform sign-in page.
2. Enter your **email** and **password**.
3. (Optional) Tick **Remember me** to stay signed in on this device.
4. Click **Sign in**.

## What you'll see

- If the credentials match, you land on the home page that fits your role:
  - **Admins and Managers** → Admin dashboard.
  - **Owners and Residents** → the shared home page today (dedicated portals are coming).
- If your credentials are wrong, an inline error appears under the email field.

## Too many attempts

If you type the wrong password several times in a row, the platform pauses sign-in attempts for a short cooldown. You'll see an **amber banner** that says how many seconds are left before you can try again. Wait for the timer to clear, then try once more.

## Accessibility

- **Keyboard**: tab moves through email → password → remember-me → sign-in. Press **Enter** inside any field to submit.
- **Show / hide password**: there's a button inside the password field. It has a clear aria-label in your chosen language so screen readers can announce its state.
- **Inline errors** are announced by screen readers as soon as they appear.
- **Language**: a language toggle in the form switches the whole page between English and Arabic. The sign-in form is fully right-to-left when Arabic is selected.

## Language

- Click the language toggle in the sign-in form to switch between English and Arabic.
- Your language choice is remembered for the session.

## Common issues

- **"These credentials do not match our records."** — the email or password is wrong. Try again; if you forget your password, use the **Forgot password?** link.
- **Amber "Too many attempts" banner** — wait the number of seconds shown, then try again. The banner counts down automatically.
- **Form doesn't submit** — check both fields are filled in and the **Sign in** button is enabled.
- **Language didn't switch** — the toggle only affects the sign-in page; your profile language preference is applied once you're signed in.
