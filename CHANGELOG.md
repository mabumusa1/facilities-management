## [Unreleased]

### Added
- Leasing — lead conversion: admins can convert a Qualified lead to an Owner or Resident contact from the lead detail page or the leads list row menu; a pre-conversion duplicate check by email or phone offers the option to link the lead to the existing contact (recommended) or create a new record; after conversion the lead status changes to Converted and a Converted Contact card with a link to the new or linked contact appears on the lead detail page; requires `leads.UPDATE` permission; concurrent conversion attempts on the same lead are blocked ([#401](https://github.com/mabumusa1/facilities-management/pull/401))
- Leasing — leads Excel import: Property Managers and Admins with the `leads.CREATE` permission can download a pre-formatted Excel template from the **Import Leads** dropdown on the Leads page, upload a filled file (up to 500 rows, 5 MB, `.xlsx` / `.xls`), and review a row-level validation preview showing total, valid, and error row counts with a per-row error table; confirming imports only the valid rows (bulk insert); errored rows are skipped and a **Download Error Report** CSV is available for download; imported leads are automatically tagged with the **Excel Import** source; re-submitting an already-imported file is rejected; imports are tenant-isolated ([#400](https://github.com/mabumusa1/facilities-management/pull/400))
- Leasing — lead detail page: admins and property managers can open any lead record from the Leads list to view full contact info, update the lead status (New / Contacted / Qualified / Converted / Lost, with optional lost-reason text), assign or unassign the lead to a team member via a search-and-select sheet, add free-text activity notes, and read a reverse-chronological activity timeline showing every status change, assignment event, and note with actor name and timestamp; deleting a lead is restricted to admins and requires confirmation; all actions are gated by `leads.VIEW` / `leads.UPDATE` RBAC permissions with tenant isolation enforced ([#399](https://github.com/mabumusa1/facilities-management/pull/399))
- Leasing — leads list: Property Managers and Admins with the leads.VIEW permission can browse all leads at **Leasing → Leads** with search (name or phone), status (New / Contacted / Qualified / Converted / Lost), source, and rows-per-page filters; the table shows lead name, phone, email, source, colour-coded status badge, assigned-to, and created date with pagination; users with leads.CREATE permission can add a lead manually via the **+ Add Lead** drawer (bilingual EN/AR name fields, phone with country code, email, source, and notes); all data is scoped to the current account ([#398](https://github.com/mabumusa1/facilities-management/pull/398))
- Leasing — lease renewal offers: Property Managers can generate a renewal offer from any active lease within 90 days of expiry, pre-filled with the current unit, tenant, rent, payment frequency, and contract type; managers adjust the new rent (with a live difference indicator), set a valid-until deadline, and add bilingual EN/AR messages; the offer is saved as a draft and sent to the tenant by email; the lease detail page shows a persistent renewal banner with the countdown, offer status badge, and contextual action buttons (Send, View Offer, Record Decision, Convert to New Lease, or Generate New Offer); managers record tenant acceptance or decline from a decision dialog; accepted offers unlock a Convert to New Lease flow pre-filled with renewal terms; a scheduled job expires offers automatically when the valid-until date passes; all offers are trackable from Leasing → Renewal Offers with status and search filters ([#397](https://github.com/mabumusa1/facilities-management/pull/397))
- Leasing — move-out workflow: Property Managers can initiate a move-out on any active lease, setting a move-out date and reason; the unit transitions to Under Maintenance automatically; a room-by-room inspection checklist captures condition (Excellent / Good / Fair / Poor), notes, and photo evidence per room; a deposit deductions calculator records itemised deductions with bilingual EN/AR labels and reasons (Damage / Cleaning / Unpaid Rent / Utility / Other); the refund amount or outstanding charge is calculated in real time; deductions exceeding the deposit are allowed with a confirmation warning; one active move-out per lease is enforced ([#396](https://github.com/mabumusa1/facilities-management/pull/396))
- Leasing — tenant notices: Property Managers and Admins with manager-level scope can send formal bilingual notices (Rent Increase, Renewal Offer, Move-Out Reminder, or Free-form) to the tenant on any active lease from a dedicated **Send Notice** page; both English and Arabic subject and body are required; an inline **Preview** toggle shows the full bilingual notice before submission; the form is blocked with a clear error when the Resident contact has no email address on file; every sent notice is permanently stored on the lease with type, bilingual subject and body, **sent_at** timestamp, and **sent_by** user; a paginated **Notice History** accordion on the same page shows all past notices with lazy-loaded body text; the **Send Notice** button on the lease detail page displays a count badge ([#395](https://github.com/mabumusa1/facilities-management/pull/395))
- Leasing — lease amendments: Property Managers and Admins with manager-level scope can amend the structural terms of an active lease (end date, total rental amount, contract type, payment schedule, security deposit, and terms and conditions) from a dedicated **Amend Lease Terms** page; a live **Diff Preview** table highlights every changed field before submission; a required reason (min 5 chars) is recorded with each amendment; the lease detail page gains a permanent **Amendment History** timeline showing each amendment's number, author, timestamp, reason, and field-level old/new values; amendment numbering increments sequentially; tenant reassignment and rent escalation are not part of this form; an optional **Generate signed addendum** checkbox records intent for the upcoming Documents addendum workflow ([#394](https://github.com/mabumusa1/facilities-management/pull/394))
- Properties — unit metadata management: unit detail page now shows Specifications (bedrooms, bathrooms, living rooms, furnished status, parking bays, view type), Amenities (badge list with empty-state fallback), and Pricing Reference (asking rent with currency and period); all fields editable from the unit Edit page; area must be greater than 0; Marketplace listings can read type, area, specifications, and asking rent without re-entry ([#392](https://github.com/mabumusa1/facilities-management/pull/392))
- Properties — bulk unit import: Property Managers and Admins can upload an `.xlsx` file of up to 500 units from the Units index page; a 4-step wizard guides through file upload, column mapping (auto-fuzzy-matched to Unit Name, Community, Building, Area (sqm), Status), row-level validation preview (required fields, area > 0, valid status, intra-file duplicates, DB duplicates), and async import execution (queued for > 50 rows, inline for ≤ 50 rows); partial imports are supported — valid rows import even when some rows fail; tenant isolation enforced — all imported units are scoped to the current account ([#391](https://github.com/mabumusa1/facilities-management/pull/391))
- Admin — user management: admins invite new users by email (invitation link valid 72 hours, resend and revoke supported); invitees set their own password via the invitation link which activates the account and verifies the email; admins can deactivate a user (immediately invalidates all sessions), reactivate, or send a password reset on their behalf; deactivate button is disabled on the admin's own account; tenant isolation enforced — admins can only manage users in their own account ([#389](https://github.com/mabumusa1/facilities-management/pull/389))
- Auth — session management: users can view all active sessions by device, browser, and location on the Security settings page; revoke unrecognized sessions with password confirmation; log out all other sessions at once; current session is clearly marked and cannot be revoked ([#387](https://github.com/mabumusa1/facilities-management/pull/387))
- Admin — platform feature flags: super-admins can enable or disable platform features per tenant from a new Features tab on the Tenant Detail page; toggles are confirmed via dialog with immediate-effect warnings; every change is audit-logged with actor and timestamp; six initial flags (Marketplace, Power BI, Facilities, Communication Hub, Document Vault, Reports & Analytics) with tier-aware inclusion defaults ([#385](https://github.com/mabumusa1/facilities-management/pull/385))
- Reports — occupancy report: per-community unit counts by status (occupied, available, maintenance, off-plan) with occupancy rate percentage ([#382](https://github.com/mabumusa1/facilities-management/pull/382))
- Reports — lease pipeline: expiring leases grouped by month with renewal counts; configurable look-ahead window up to 24 months ([#382](https://github.com/mabumusa1/facilities-management/pull/382))
