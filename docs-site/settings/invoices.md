# Invoice Templates

Customize the appearance and content of invoices generated for service requests and transactions.

## Accessing Invoice Settings

Navigate to **Settings → Invoice**.

## Configuring Your Invoice Template

| Section | What You Can Customize |
|---------|----------------------|
| **Logo** | Upload your company logo |
| **Header** | Company name, address, contact details |
| **Color Scheme** | Primary color for headers and borders |
| **Footer Text** | Legal disclaimers, payment terms, thank-you message |
| **Invoice Number Format** | Prefix and numbering pattern (e.g., INV-2025-001) |
| **Payment Terms** | Default due date rule (e.g., "Net 30") |
| **Tax Settings** | VAT or other tax labels and rates |

## Preview

Click **Preview** to see a sample invoice with your current settings before saving.

## Saving the Template

Click **Save** to apply your settings. All future invoices will use the updated template.

::: tip
Keep your logo file under 2MB and use PNG or SVG format for best quality on printed invoices.
:::
