# Tracking Service Requests

## Viewing Your Requests

Navigate to **Requests** to see all your service requests. Click any request to open the detail view.

## Request Status Meanings

| Status | What It Means | What to Expect |
|--------|---------------|----------------|
| **New** | Submitted, awaiting assignment | A manager will review and assign a professional |
| **Assigned** | A professional has been assigned | The professional will contact you to schedule |
| **Rescheduled** | The visit was rescheduled | Check the new date/time |
| **Request Accepted** | Professional confirmed the appointment | Await the scheduled visit |
| **In Progress** | Work is underway | Professional is on-site |
| **Quote Sent** | Professional sent a cost estimate | Review and approve or reject the quote |
| **Invoice Created** | Work is done; invoice submitted | Review and accept or dispute the invoice |
| **Resolved** | Issue fully resolved | Rate your experience |
| **Canceled** | Request was canceled | Check notes for reason |
| **Rejected** | Request was declined | Contact your manager for alternatives |

## Request Detail Page

The detail page shows the complete history of a request:

### Overview
- Title and description
- Category and subcategory
- Unit / community
- Priority
- Current status

### Timeline
A chronological list of every status change with timestamps and notes.

### Professional Details
When assigned:
- Professional's name
- Scheduled visit date and time
- Contact information (if visible per configuration)

### Quote / Invoice
When a quote or invoice exists:
- Itemized cost breakdown
- Approve or reject buttons

### Photos / Documents
Any photos submitted with the request or uploaded by the professional.

## Approving or Rejecting a Quote

When the professional sends a cost estimate:

1. Open the request detail page
2. Review the **Quote** section
3. Click **Approve Quote** to proceed or **Reject Quote** to decline
4. If rejected, add a reason in the notes

## Approving or Disputing an Invoice

After the work is complete:

1. Open the request detail page
2. Review the **Invoice** section
3. Click **Accept Invoice** to close the request as resolved
4. Click **Reject Invoice** if you dispute the work or cost

::: info
Accepting the invoice marks the request as **Resolved** and closes it.
:::

## Canceling a Request

You can cancel a request while it is in **New** or **Assigned** status:
1. Open the request
2. Click **Cancel Request**
3. Enter a reason
4. Click **Confirm**
