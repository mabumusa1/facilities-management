# Managing Leases

## Viewing All Leases

Navigate to **Leasing → Leases** to see all contracts. Use the filters to narrow the list:

- **Status** – Active, Draft, Expired, Canceled, Closed
- **Community / Building**
- **Date range** – start or end date
- **Search** – by tenant name, contract number, or unit

## Lease Detail Page

Click any lease to open its detail view:

### Summary Section
- Contract number and status badge
- Unit and community details
- Tenant name with a link to their contact record
- Lease term (start and end dates)
- Monthly rent and payment schedule

### Transactions Tab
All financial records tied to this lease:
- Rent charges and payments
- Late fees
- Deposits

### Documents Tab
Uploaded files for this lease (contract PDF, tenant ID, etc.)

### History Tab
A complete audit trail of every action taken on this lease.

## Lease Actions

Depending on the lease status, you can perform the following actions from the detail page:

### Terminate a Lease (Early Exit)

If a tenant needs to leave before the end date:
1. Open the lease
2. Click **Terminate**
3. Enter the **termination date** and **reason**
4. Optionally record any early termination fees
5. Click **Confirm**

The lease status changes to **Canceled**.

### Move Out

When a tenant vacates at the end of the lease:
1. Open the lease
2. Click **Move Out**
3. Confirm the move-out date
4. Record any final charges or deposit deductions
5. Click **Confirm**

The lease status changes to **Closed** and the unit returns to **Available**.

### Edit Lease Details

Minor edits (e.g., updating contact info or adding documents) can be done while the lease is **Draft**. Once active, most fields are locked for audit purposes.

## Expiring Leases

Navigate to **Leasing → Expiring Leases** to see all leases ending in the next 30, 60, or 90 days. This view helps you proactively reach out to tenants about renewal.

## Overdue Leases

Navigate to **Leasing → Overdue** to see leases with unpaid rent or fees past their due date. From here you can:
- Record a manual payment
- Send a reminder notification to the tenant
- Escalate the overdue balance
