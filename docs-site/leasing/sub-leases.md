# Sub-Leases

A **sub-lease** is an arrangement where a primary tenant sub-lets their unit (or part of it) to another person (the sub-tenant).

## Viewing Sub-Leases

Navigate to **Leasing → Sub-Leases** to see all active sub-lease arrangements.

## Creating a Sub-Lease

::: info Prerequisite
A primary lease must already be active for the unit. Sub-leases cannot be created on vacant units.
:::

1. Open the primary lease for the unit
2. Click **+ Add Sub-Lease**
3. Fill in the sub-tenant's details:
   - Name, email, phone
   - National ID
4. Set the sub-lease terms:
   - Start and end date (must be within the primary lease period)
   - Monthly amount
5. Upload any supporting documents
6. Click **Save**

## Managing Sub-Leases

Sub-leases have their own status tracking:
- **Active** – the sub-tenant is in residence
- **Expired** – the sub-lease term ended
- **Canceled** – terminated early

You can view the sub-lease detail page to see transactions and history specific to that sub-lease.

## Terminating a Sub-Lease

Open the sub-lease and click **Terminate**. Enter the termination date and reason, then confirm.

::: warning
Terminating a sub-lease does **not** affect the primary lease. The primary tenant remains responsible for the unit.
:::
