# Contacts Overview

The Contacts module is the central directory for all people in your system — property owners, tenants, administrative staff, and service professionals.

## Contact Types

| Type | Description | Link |
|------|-------------|------|
| **Owners** | People who own units or properties | [Manage Owners](/contacts/owners) |
| **Tenants** | Residents currently occupying units under a lease | [Manage Tenants](/contacts/tenants) |
| **Admins & Staff** | Your internal team with platform access | [Manage Admins](/contacts/admins) |
| **Service Professionals** | External contractors and maintenance staff | [Manage Professionals](/contacts/professionals) |

## Accessing Contacts

Navigate to **Contacts** in the left menu. The sidebar lets you switch between contact types.

## Contact Statistics

Navigate to **Contacts → Statistics** for an overview:
- Total contacts by type
- New contacts added this month
- Active vs. inactive contacts
- Tenants with pending invitations
