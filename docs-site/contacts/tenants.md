# Tenants

**Tenants** are residents who occupy units under a lease agreement.

## Viewing Tenants

Navigate to **Contacts → Tenants** to see all tenant records. Filter by:
- Community or building
- Lease status (active, expired)
- Invitation status (invited, accepted)

## Adding a Tenant

Tenants are typically created during the [lease creation process](/leasing/create). You can also add tenants independently:

1. Go to **Contacts → Tenants → + New Tenant**
2. Fill in:

| Field | Required |
|-------|----------|
| First Name | ✅ |
| Last Name | ✅ |
| Email | ✅ |
| Phone | ✅ |
| National ID | ✅ |
| Nationality | Optional |

3. Click **Save**

## Inviting a Tenant

After creating a tenant, you can send them an **invitation** to create their account and access the tenant portal:

1. Open the tenant record
2. Click **Send Invitation**
3. The tenant receives an email with a link to set up their account

The tenant's record shows invitation status:
- **Not Invited** – invitation not yet sent
- **Invited** – email sent, awaiting registration
- **Active** – tenant has created their account

## Tenant Details

Click a tenant name to open their record:

### Overview
- Personal information and contact details
- Invitation and account status

### Leases Tab
- Active and past lease contracts
- Current unit details

### Service Requests Tab
- All maintenance requests submitted by this tenant

### Transactions Tab
- Rent payments and outstanding balances

### Family Members Tab
- Registered family members (co-occupants)

## Managing Family Members

Tenants can have family members registered in the system as co-occupants:

1. Open the tenant record
2. Click **Family Members** tab
3. Click **+ Add Family Member**
4. Enter the family member's details
5. Click **Save**

## Editing a Tenant

Open the tenant record and click **Edit**. Update the fields and save.
