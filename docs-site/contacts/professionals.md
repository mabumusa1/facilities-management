# Service Professionals

**Service Professionals** are external contractors, technicians, and service providers who handle maintenance and repair requests.

## Viewing Professionals

Navigate to **Contacts → Service Professionals** to see all registered professionals.

## Adding a Service Professional

1. Navigate to **Contacts → Service Professionals → + New Professional**
2. Fill in:

| Field | Required |
|-------|----------|
| First Name | ✅ |
| Last Name | ✅ |
| Email | ✅ |
| Phone | ✅ |
| Specialty / Trade | Optional |
| Company | Optional |

3. Click **Save**

The professional receives an invitation email to access the service professional portal.

## Professional Details

Click a professional's name to see:
- Personal and contact information
- **Assigned Requests** – open and in-progress service requests
- **History** – completed requests with ratings and invoices

## Assigning Requests to Professionals

Professionals are assigned to service requests by a Service Manager or Admin. See [Assigning Professionals](/service-requests/assign) for the step-by-step workflow.

## Service Categories

Professionals can be associated with specific service categories (e.g., Plumbing, Electrical, HVAC). This helps managers quickly find the right person for each request.

::: tip
Well-categorized professionals lead to faster assignment and resolution times.
:::
