# Lease Statistics Report

The Lease Statistics report gives you a high-level view of leasing activity and trends.

## Accessing the Report

Navigate to **Reports → Lease Statistics**.

## Metrics Available

### Lease Counts
- Total active leases
- New leases created in the period
- Leases renewed in the period
- Leases expired or terminated

### Revenue
- Total contracted rent value
- Average rent per unit
- Rent by unit type (residential vs. commercial)

### Trends
- Month-over-month lease count changes
- Revenue trend line
- Renewal rate

### Lease Status Breakdown
A pie or bar chart showing the distribution of leases by status (Active, Draft, Expired, Canceled, Closed).

## Filters

| Filter | Options |
|--------|---------|
| Date Range | Custom range or presets (This Month, Last Month, This Year, etc.) |
| Community | All or specific community |
| Building | All or specific building |
| Unit Type | Residential, Commercial |
| Lease Status | Active, Expired, etc. |

## Interpreting the Report

- **High renewal rate** (>70%) indicates tenant satisfaction
- **Increasing average rent** suggests healthy market demand
- **Many draft leases** may indicate process bottlenecks in activation
