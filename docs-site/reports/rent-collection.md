# Rent Collection Report

The Rent Collection Report tracks payment performance — how much rent is expected, how much has been collected, and what remains outstanding.

## Accessing the Report

Navigate to **Reports → Rent Collection**.

## Metrics Available

### Collection Summary
- **Expected** – total rent due in the period
- **Collected** – total payments received
- **Outstanding** – remaining unpaid
- **Collection rate** – percentage collected on time

### By Month
Month-by-month chart of expected vs. collected rent.

### Overdue Analysis
- Tenants with overdue balances
- Age of overdue amounts (30/60/90/120+ days)
- Total overdue value

### By Community / Building
Collection rate broken down by property to identify underperforming segments.

## Filters

| Filter | Options |
|--------|---------|
| Date Range | Custom or preset periods |
| Community | All or specific |
| Building | All or specific |
| Status | Paid, Due, Overdue |

## Export

Click **Export** to download the report as Excel or PDF. The Excel export includes individual lease-level detail rows for reconciliation.

## Using This Report

- **Collection rate below 95%** warrants a review of follow-up processes
- **Concentration of overdue in one building** may signal specific tenant issues
- Use the overdue detail to prioritize collection efforts
