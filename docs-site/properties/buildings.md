# Buildings

A **building** is a physical structure within a community — a residential tower, villa cluster, or commercial block.

## Viewing Buildings

Navigate to **Properties → Buildings** to see all buildings, or open a community and view its buildings from the **Buildings** tab.

## Creating a Building

1. Go to **Properties → Buildings** or open a community and click **+ New Building**
2. Fill in the required fields:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Community** | The community this building belongs to |
| **Name** | Building name or number |
| **Address** | Street address |
| **Building Type** | Residential or Commercial |
| **Number of Floors** | Total floor count |

3. Click **Save**

### Bulk Upload Buildings

To create multiple buildings at once:
1. Navigate to **Properties → Buildings**
2. Click **Bulk Upload**
3. Download the Excel template, fill it in, and re-upload

## Building Details

Click a building to open its detail page, which shows:
- **Units** list with occupancy status
- **Building statistics** (occupancy rate, revenue summary)
- **Active leases** in this building
- **Service requests** linked to this building

## Managing Units Within a Building

From the building detail page, click the **Units** tab to:
- See all units and their current status
- Click a unit to open its details
- Add new units to this building

## Editing or Deactivating a Building

Open the building and click **Edit**. You can update all fields and change the status between **Active** and **Inactive**.
