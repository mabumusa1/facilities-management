# System Overview

The **Facilities Management System** is a comprehensive, cloud-based platform for managing residential and commercial properties. It covers the full property lifecycle — from listing and leasing units to handling maintenance requests and tracking financial transactions.

## Who Is This For?

The system supports four types of users:

| User Type | Who They Are |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Admin / Staff** | Property managers, accounting staff, service coordinators, and marketing teams who operate the platform |
| **Owner** | Property owners who want visibility into their units, leases, and financials |
| **Tenant** | Residents who need to submit service requests, book facilities, or track their lease details |
| **Service Professional** | Contractors and maintenance staff who receive and resolve service requests |

## Core Modules

### 🏢 Properties
Organize your portfolio in a three-level hierarchy:
- **Communities** – top-level property groupings (e.g., a residential complex)
- **Buildings** – physical structures within a community
- **Units** – individual apartments, villas, or commercial spaces

### 📋 Leasing
Manage the full rental lifecycle:
- Draft and activate lease contracts
- Handle lease applications with review and approval flows
- Renew expiring leases
- Process move-outs and terminations

### 🔧 Service Requests
End-to-end maintenance management:
- Submit home and common area service requests
- Assign licensed professionals
- Track status from assignment through invoicing and resolution

### 💰 Transactions
Complete financial tracking:
- Record rent payments, fees, and expenses
- Manage a chart of accounts and journal entries
- Track overdue balances per tenant

### 👥 Contacts
Centralized contact management:
- Owners, tenants, admins, and service professionals
- Invitation system for new tenants
- Family member records per tenant

### 📊 Reports
Analytical insights:
- Lease statistics and trends
- Occupancy rates by building or community
- Maintenance request metrics
- Rent collection summaries

### 🗓️ Facilities & Visitors
Community amenity management:
- Facility booking requests with approval workflows
- Visitor access permits and check-in/check-out tracking
- Community directory

### 📢 Announcements
Keep residents informed with community-wide announcements.

### ⚙️ Settings
Configure service categories, forms, invoice templates, and bank details.
