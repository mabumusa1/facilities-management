# Logging In

## Accessing the System

Open your web browser and navigate to the URL provided by your system administrator.

## Login Page

Enter your **email address** and **password**, then click **Log In**.

::: tip Forgot your password?
Click **Forgot Password?** on the login page. Enter your registered email address and you will receive a password reset link within a few minutes.
:::

## Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

If your organization has enabled two-factor authentication, you will be prompted for a verification code after entering your password.

1. Open your authenticator app (e.g., Google Authenticator, Authy)
2. Find the entry for the Facilities Management System
3. Enter the **6-digit code** displayed in the app
4. Click **Verify**

### Lost your authenticator?

If you cannot access your authenticator app, click **Use a recovery code** and enter one of the recovery codes you saved when you set up 2FA. Contact your administrator if you have lost all recovery codes.

## Email Verification

New accounts require email verification before accessing the system. Check your inbox for a verification email and click the confirmation link. If you did not receive the email, click **Resend Verification Email** on the verification page.

## Logging Out

Click your profile avatar in the top-right corner and select **Log Out**. Your session will be terminated immediately.

::: warning
Always log out when using a shared or public computer.
:::

## Common Issues

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Invalid email or password | Double-check your credentials. Passwords are case-sensitive. |
| Account locked | Contact your system administrator to unlock your account. |
| No access after login | Your account type may not have access to the requested section. Check with your admin. |
| Page shows "403 Forbidden" | Your role does not have permission for that section. See [Roles & Permissions](/guide/roles). |
